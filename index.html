Who are we?

One Stop Auto Solution began as a small company, two-bay shop in the heart of the neighborhood. What started as a one-man operation has grown into one of the area’s most respected names in auto care.

The founder, Irfan Chowdury, built the garage on a simple mission: “Deliver honest, affordable, and expert car repair service — and treat every customer like family.”

With over 15 years of experience, Irfan and his team are passionate about helping drivers stay safe on the road.
